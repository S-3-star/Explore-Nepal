Top Tourist Attractions in Nepal

Pokhara

Pokhara is a city of lakes and natural beauty. It offers views of the Annapurna range, boating in Phewa Lake, and adventure sports like paragliding.

Chitwan National Park

Located in southern Nepal, this UNESCO World Heritage Site is famous for jungle safaris, one-horned rhinos, elephants, and bird watching.

Everest Base Camp

One of the most iconic trekking destinations in the world, EBC offers an unforgettable journey to the base of Mount Everest.

Lumbini

Lumbini is the birthplace of Lord Buddha. It is a peaceful pilgrimage site featuring ancient ruins, monasteries, and the sacred Maya Devi Temple.

Bhaktapur & Patan

These ancient cities are rich in Newar architecture, temples, courtyards, and traditional crafts. A must-visit for heritage lovers.
