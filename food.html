Traditional Foods of Nepal

Dal Bhat

The everyday meal of Nepali people – rice, lentil soup, and curry, often served with pickles and greens.

Momo

Steamed dumplings filled with meat or vegetables – a must-try street food across Nepal.

Sel Roti

Ring-shaped rice bread, deep-fried and crispy, traditionally prepared during festivals like Tihar and Dashain.

Gundruk

Fermented leafy greens, often prepared as a soup or side dish – a unique traditional Nepali flavor.

Yomari

Sweet steamed dumplings filled with jaggery and sesame seeds, a specialty of the Newar community during festivals.

Chatamari

Also called Nepali pizza, this rice flour crepe is topped with meat, eggs, and vegetables – popular in Newar cuisine.
