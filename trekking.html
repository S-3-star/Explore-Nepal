Famous Trekking Routes in Nepal

Everest Base Camp Trek

Follow the footsteps of climbers to the base of the world's tallest peak, with scenic Sherpa villages and glaciers.

Annapurna Circuit

A diverse trek through mountain passes, local villages, and lush forests—one of the most popular treks in Asia.

Langtang Valley Trek

Known for its natural beauty and fewer crowds, this trek is perfect for beginners and offers a close look at local culture.
