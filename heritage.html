Cultural Heritage of Nepal

Durbar Squares

Kathmandu, Bhaktapur, and Patan Durbar Squares are UNESCO World Heritage Sites showcasing Newar architecture, palaces, and temples.

Pashupatinath Temple

A sacred Hindu temple on the banks of the Bagmati River, attracting thousands of devotees and tourists yearly.

Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple)

One of the oldest religious sites in Nepal, offering panoramic views of Kathmandu Valley.
